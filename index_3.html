🏭 About Us

Crafting Hydraulic Solutions Since Day One

Scrupulous Uniotech is a Nagpur-based hydraulic engineering company specialising in the design, manufacture, customisation and repair of hydraulic pumps — serving agriculture, construction, mining and heavy industry.

  • In-house manufacturing facility with precision CNC machining
  • Custom hydraulic pumps built exactly to your specifications
  • Authorised dealership for leading hydraulic component brands
  • Experienced team of hydraulic engineers and technicians
  • Rigorous quality and pressure testing before every delivery
  • After-sales support and preventive maintenance services
